about

NO CLASS STUDIO is a one woman show.

With a strong passion for visual and applied arts, I focus on creating thoughtful, strategic design solutions that seamlessly blend concept and aesthetics. I explore unique visual languages, resisting the easiest route of mindlesly following latest design trends, in favor of intentional design choices that feel both timeless and contemporary. Through brand identities, logos, packaging, and illustration, my work reflects a balance between creative experimentation and clear visual communication, always driven by curiosity, growth, and a commitment to strong design fundamentals.
