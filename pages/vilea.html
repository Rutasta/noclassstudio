POTTERY STUDIO VISUAL IDENTITY

Brand Identity, Logo Design, Packaging, Creative Direction

Vilea is a contemporary pottery brand crafting both ceramic vessels and clay jewelry with bold character and emotional texture. My role as a designer was to create a distinctive visual language that would be representative of lively Vilea community, as well as to help the brand to present itself as a new, original and bold new name in the pottery and clay design world.
